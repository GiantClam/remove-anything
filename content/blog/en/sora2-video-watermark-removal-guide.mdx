## Understanding Sora2 Video Watermarks

### What are Sora2 Watermarks?

Sora2 watermarks are visual markers embedded in videos generated by OpenAI's Sora2 model. These watermarks typically appear as:

- **Text overlays**: "Sora2" or similar branding text
- **Logo elements**: Company or platform logos
- **Corner markers**: Small watermarks in video corners
- **Semi-transparent overlays**: Subtle branding elements throughout the video
